Follow these steps:

1. open file explorer

2. goto %AppData%\Microsoft\Windows\Themes

3. open another file explorer window

4. split your screen

5. copy your image to the themes folder(make sure its png or jpg)
6. delete the CachedFiles folder
7. rename your image to TranscodedWallpaper
8. Open taskmanager
9. Click the down arrow and then file
10. Now type the command:
taskkill /f /im explorer.exe, click it to copy it
11. Now do the same, But type the command:
explorer.exe, click it to copy it Now check your background!
